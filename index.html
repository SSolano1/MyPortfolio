About Me

Hi! I’m Solon Solano an energetic, hard-working Full Stack developer who doesn’t mind putting in the time to get things done right. I’ve worked full-time, along with a part-time job all while getting my education. Now while earning my second degree in Information Technology, I attended the UCF Coding BootCamp and learned the necessary skills to become an effective Full Stack Developer. I am proficient in HTML/CSS, Javascript, MySQL, and the MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js) stack. I am a strategic leader that pays attention to detail and faces challenges head on and anticipates issues before they arise. I am always striving to find new ways to improve efficiency while minimizing risk and fostering a culture of compliance. I want to work with spirited, fun people who want to accomplish and create great things. I believe the difference between who you are and whom you want to be is what you do and that your best life imaginable is waiting for you, so work hard and claim it! So if you would like to find out more about my work or would like to discuss them personally, please contact me.
